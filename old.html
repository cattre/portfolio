A little bit about me.

Having looked over many a developer's shoulder during a ten-year career managing software projects, I finally decided to get stuck in myself and have embarked on a full stack software development course.

Originally from London, we moved to Bath in 2016 and have been enjoying the delights of the South West ever since. Our next move is now calling and we will be returning to my wife's birthplace on the Cornwall coast.

I try to get out for a run every now and then, although prefer a bit of kayaking and am therefore looking forward to making the most of being near the sea. I may even embarass myself on a surfboard. Our dog loves beaches/forests/fields and frequently takes us on adventures, which happily often end up at a pub.

I am still learning the ropes at the moment but should be available for my first real challenge in early 2021.
